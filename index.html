Project Snapshots

DNA Alignment XY

In 2014, researchers combined two artificial codons into the genetic code of a viable, self-replicating organism. This project takes the local and global sequence alignment algorithms and adapts them to use separate scoring strategies for natural and artificial codons. Requires Silverlight.

MinesweeperJS

SpectrumToRGB

Have you ever wondered what a pigment looks like if you purified a macroscopic amount? SpectrumToRGB takes an input absorbance or reflectance spectrum and approximates the color in RGB space.
